Rujun Han

Senior Research Scientist at Google

I work on LLM Agents and post-training at Google. My recent work on Deep Research is now available on Gemini Enterprise and Gemini DR Max with millions of users. Previously, I spent two years at AWS AI Labs leading research in RAG, LLM evaluations, and alignment while building enterprise AI assistant services Kendra and Amazon Q.

I earned my CS Ph.D. from USC, advised by Nanyun Peng and Aram Galstyan. My thesis proposes event-centric reasoning with a wide range of AI applications. Prior to USC, I worked as a statistical researcher and data scientist, and obtained an M.Sc. in Data Science from NYU where I worked with Kyunghyun Cho.
